Welcome to the eRules search engine - the eRSE provides a capability for you to search against the list of rules and regulations that the EPA is proposing in order to enforce legislation that has been recently passed by Congress. Each of these rules is open for comment and the EPA wants your input! Through this tool you can expand the results and determine how they're related to the Clean Air Act, as well as see linkages from the proposed rules to programs that might be related.
